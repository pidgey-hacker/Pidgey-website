Ingredients

Note:- you can just skip the making of a main ingredient if you aldready have it, like premade pizza/tomato sauce or premade pizza base

i would recommend you to buy a premade tomato/pizza sauce for your first time as it is really convineint and easy, but you can make your own by following this tutorial as well. :)

Pizza/tomato Sauce:-

  • 800g Tomatoes
  • 10ml Olive oil
  • 20g Unsalted butter
  • 15g Garlic
  • 80g Onion
  • 50ml Water
  • 1Tsp Salt
  • 18g Sugar
  • 50g Ketchup
  • 1Tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1Tsp Dried Basil
  • Black pepper

Pizza dough:-

  • 1Tsp Instant dry yeast
  • 10g Sugar
  • 100ml Warm water
  • Bread flour
  • 1/2Tsp Salt
  • Olive oil
  • 100g Pizza/Tomato sauce
  • toppings (anything you want, i reccoment Black olives, onion, tomatoes and maybe even salamai)

Procedure to make the pizza

Pizza dough:-

step 1:-

take a bowl and add 1Tsp(tea spoon) instant dry yeast, then add 10 grams(almost a teaspoon) of sugar and add 100ml of warm water and mix it thourougly (properly). then after mixing wait for 10 mins
