This page will show you how to cook *literally* perfect pizza at home.

Ingredients

Note:- you can just skip the making of a main ingredient if you aldready have it, like premade pizza/tomato sauce or a premade pizza base/dough

i would recommend you to buy a premade tomato/pizza sauce for your first time as it is really convineint and easy, but you can make your own by following this tutorial as well.

Pizza/tomato Sauce:-

  • 800g Tomatoes
  • 10ml Olive oil
  • 20g Unsalted butter
  • 15g Garlic
  • 80g Onion
  • 50ml Water
  • 1Tsp Salt
  • 18g Sugar
  • 50g Ketchup
  • 1Tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1Tsp Dried Basil
  • Black pepper

Pizza dough:-

  • 400g Flour (Basic Wheat flour)
  • 200ml lukewarm water
  • 7g Dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 spoon of olive oil
  • a little bit of sugar (to feed the yeast)

Procedure to make the pizza dough

step 1:-

combine all ingredients all in a bowl and knead to a dough.

step 2:-

let it rest for 30 min in a luke warm place.

step 3:-

then flour the work surface and roll it out thinly
